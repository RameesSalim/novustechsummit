About NOVUS

Novus is a 3 day annual technology innovation and entrepreneurship summit of SCMS SSET. Novus is conducted jointly by the SSET student bodies of ASME, IEEE, IEDC and eCell. Through this event we aim to promote innovation and startup culture in colleges and bring students closer to engineering and technology.

About SSET

SCMS School of Engineering and Technology (SSET) is envisaged as a premier institution offering technology related education of exceptional quality to students by developing their total personality with due emphasis on ethical values and preparing them to meet the growing challenges of the industry and diverse societal needs of the institution.

Our Vision

Novus was born as part of a mission to promote innovation and develop a start-up culture among students across the nation. It is designed to be an event focussed on emerging technologies and entrepreneurship with a far reaching vision to upskill students and act as a platform for young minds to converge, network and exchange their ideas. Novus desires to be a humble contributor in helping India achieve the next wave of technology powered growth and empower millions of youth who are expected to enter the workforce in the coming decades.
